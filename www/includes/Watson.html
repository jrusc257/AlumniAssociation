Watson Residence Hall
 

Dedicated to Charles F. Watson, who was the Head of the Geography Department from 1913 to 1946. The building was constructed in 1968 for a cost of $964,000. During his life, Watson was the author of various textbooks on the geography of Wisconsin and contributed articles to geographical and navigational publications. The first floor hosts male students, while the second floor hosts female students. On the third and fourth floors, men and women are assigned to rooms on a “scattered” basis.
