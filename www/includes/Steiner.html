Steiner Residence Hall
 

Steiner Hall is a 275 bed residence facility initially for men is located at 401 Isadore Street, and was named in honor of Herbert Steiner. Herbert was a faculty member from 1918-1955. He was dean of men and was disciplined in the areas of history and civics. Steiner Hall was built in 1967 at a cost of $838,943. The building consists of 36,229 square feet.
