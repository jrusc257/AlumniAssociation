Waste Education Center
Check In!
 

Located north of the George Stein Building and the running track, the Waste Education Center is a state-of-the-art facility that provides students with landfill, wastewater treatment, recycling, compositing, and hazardous waste management training. The building includes a small-scale wastewater treatment plant. A portion of the building also functions as the campus materials recycling facility and handles cans, bottles, plastic, and cardboard.
