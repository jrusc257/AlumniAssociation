Smith Hall
 

E.T. Smith was appointed acting president in 1939 after the abrupt resignation of Dr. Phillip H. Falk, making him the sixth president of Stevens Point Normal School in August of 1939. His short term as president ended tragically when he died suddenly on September 28th, 1940 of a bronchial ailment. Smith had been on staff since 1909. This residence hall was built in 1964 at 409 Reserve Street, at a cost of $619,724, and is 36,524 square feet.
