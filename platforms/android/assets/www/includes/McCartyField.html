McCarty Memorial Field
 

Diane McCarty studied at UW-Stevens Point, majoring in English and physical education. While enrolled, she pitched for the university fast-pitch softball team and compiled a 10-4-1 record with a 1.49 overall earned run average for two seasons. McCarty died on August 2, 1984 of myocardiopathy, a deterioration of the heart muscle, while playing softball in Wisconsin Rapids.
